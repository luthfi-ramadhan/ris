Annisa Darmawahyuni received the Ph.D. degree in computer science from the Faculty of Engineering, Universitas Sriwijaya, Indonesia. She is currently a Lecturer and a Researcher with the Intelligent System Research Group, Universitas Sriwijaya. Her research interests include biomedical engineering and deep learning.
